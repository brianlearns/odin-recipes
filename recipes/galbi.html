Galbijjim Golden Recipe

Description

This is the recipe my grandma used to make when I was growing up. A delectable combination of beef and soy sauce. Perfect with rice!

Ingredients

  • Beef rib
  • Soy sauce
  • Cooking wine
  • Sugar
  • Rice cake (37)

Steps

  1. Clean beef with water until blood runs clear
  2. Cook beef in water for 7 minutes
  3. Empty water and then replace with soy sauce
  4. Cook on medium heat for 12 additional minutes
